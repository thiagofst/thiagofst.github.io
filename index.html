Thiago Ferreira dos Santos

About Me

I am a second-year PhD student in Astronomy at Yale University.

My research focuses on exoplanet detection and characterisation, the formation and evolution of brown dwarfs, white dwarfs, wide main-sequence binaries, the star-planet connection, cataclysmic, eclipsing, and pulsating variable stars, as well as data mining of large astronomical surveys.
