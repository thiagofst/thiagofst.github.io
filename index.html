About Me

Began my studies in Brazil. Left for the US in 2023 to pursue a PhD at Yale University. Completed my MSc in 2023 (IAG/USP-BR) and BSc (UFSC-BR) in 2021.

I am a second-year PhD student in Astrophysics at Yale University. My research interests broadly include:

  • Stellar Astrophysics: Population III, spectroscopy, stellar abundances, solar analogues, blue stragglers, binary and close binary stars, stellar oscillations, stellar activity, and white dwarfs.
  • Galactic Archaeology: surveys of stellar populations, the Galactic bulge and disc, stellar content, and infrared observations of stars.
  • Exoplanets and Brown Dwarfs: formation, dynamics, evolution, detection methods (radial velocity, transit photometry), hot Neptunes, and wide binary systems.
