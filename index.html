Thiago Ferreira dos Santos

About Me

I am a first-year PhD student in Astronomy at Yale University.

My research focuses on exoplanet detection and characterisation, the formation of brown dwarfs, white dwarf-brown dwarf binaries, star-planet connections, cataclysmic, eclipsing, and pulsating variable stars, and data mining of large astronomical surveys.
