About Me

I am a first year PhD student in Astronomy at Yale University.

My research focus on exoplanets detection and characterisation, brown dwarfs formation, white dwarfs-brown dwarfs binaries, star-planet connection, cataclysmic/eclipsing/pulsating variable stars, and data mining of large astronomical surveys.
