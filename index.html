About

I am a second-year PhD student in Astrophysics at Yale University.

Research interests include:
  • Exoplanets & Brown Dwarfs: formation, dynamics, evolution, detection methods (radial velocity, transit photometry), hot Neptunes, and wide binary systems.
  • Stellar Astrophysics: spectroscopy, stellar abundances and atmospheres, solar analogues, blue stragglers, binary and close binary stars, stellar oscillations, stellar activity, and white dwarfs.
  • Galactic Archaeology: surveys of stellar populations, the Galactic bulge and disk, stellar content, and infrared observations of stars.
