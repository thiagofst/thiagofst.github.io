About Me

Born and raised in Brazil. Left for the US in 2023 to pursue a PhD in Astrophysics at Yale University. MSc in Astronomy (IAG/USP, 2023), BSc in Physics (UFSC, 2021).

I am a second-year PhD student. My research interests broadly include:

  • Stellar Astrophysics: Population III, spectroscopy, stellar abundances, oscillations, activity, solar analogues/twins, blue stragglers, white dwarfs, wide and close binary systems.
  • Galactic Structure: stellar surveys, the Galactic bulge and disc, stellar content, and infrared observations within the broader context of Galactic archaeology.
  • Exoplanets and Brown Dwarfs: formation and evolution, dynamics, wide binaries, star-planet interactions, detection techniques (radial velocity, transit photometry), and atmospheric escape.
